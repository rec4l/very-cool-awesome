I'm not sure that I have many regrets for this project! I felt that this project was one that I'm actually quite proud of. 

One regret I have is that I wish I planned better for future gamemodes, like 2v2. I originally started with 1v1 (I even knew I wanted to expand in the future) but I didn't plan with that in mind, and it wasted some time trying to refactor the codebase to allow for it.

I think one thing I would have liked to design for is custom maps, I found that the map schema I used was too restrictive and due to lack of time I didn't push for more custom maps. Instead I leaned more into the mechanics and small bug fixes to improve the feel of the game which I felt was more important.
